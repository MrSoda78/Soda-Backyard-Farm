Growing Calendar

This calendar gives a general idea of what is growing, when produce may become available, and when orders are typically being accepted. Availability may vary depending on weather and growing conditions.

April

  • Garden beds prepared
  • Seedlings started
  • Early planting begins

May

  • Lettuce may become available
  • Kale begins growing strongly
  • Limited early orders may open

June

  • Lettuce and kale more available
  • Herbs begin coming in
  • Orders accepted more regularly

July

  • Carrots may be ready
  • Garlic harvest begins
  • Peak growing season starts

August

  • Steady harvests continue
  • More produce available
  • Regular orders likely open

September

  • Late season crops continue
  • Herbs and select produce available
  • Harvest season winding down
